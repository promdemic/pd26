## **2\. Logistics & Transportation**

### **The Ferry Strategy (Edmonds/Kingston)**

- **Foot Passengers Only:** To avoid vehicle queues, all participants will travel as foot passengers.
- **Ticket Prep:** Students will purchase individual foot passenger tickets at the Edmonds terminal kiosks. Meeting at 4:30 PM ensures everyone has their tickets and is ready for the **5:25 PM** sailing.

### **The "Shuttle Squad" Timing**

- **Evening Pickup:** The ferry is scheduled to dock at 5:46 PM. The Shuttle Squad should depart the house at **5:35 PM** to be parked and waiting at the passenger off-ramp by 5:45 PM.
- **Morning Drop-off:** For the 11:10 AM return ferry, shuttles should depart the house no later than **10:40 AM** to allow for unloading and passenger boarding.

### **Overnight & Sleeping Logistics**

- **Optional Stay:** Space is designated for sleeping bags and air mattresses in the main living areas.
- **Gear List:** Kids should bring a sleeping bag, pillow, and overnight bag. These will be stored in a "Gear Room" upon arrival.
- **Morning Breakfast:** A pancake and fruit breakfast is served at 9:00 AM for all overnight guests.

## **3\. Food & Entertainment**

### **Dinner: "Coastal Chic" Buffet**

- **Main:** Grilled Salmon, Herb Roasted Chicken, and Vegetarian Pasta.
- **Sides:** Summer Corn Salad, Roasted Potatoes, and local Kingston sourdough.
- **Dessert:** "Promdemic" cupcakes and a beachside S'mores station.

### **The Music**

- **DJ Setup:** Professional DJ with outdoor-capable sound.
- **The Playlist:** Collaborative Spotify link managed via the RSVP app.

## **4\. Parent Volunteer Roles**

- **Ferry Chaperones:** 2 parents to ride the ferry with the group from Edmonds.
- **Shuttle Drivers:** 4 parents to handle the Kingston terminal loops (Friday evening and Saturday morning).
- **Food Service:** 4 parents to manage the buffet and clearing.
- **Breakfast Cooks:** 2 parents for the morning pancake station.
- **Cleanup Crew:** 4 parents for the final Saturday morning sweep.
